How to Play!
  • To start, you are given a random number.
  • There are four crystals to select from below. Clicking on a crystal will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  • In order to win the game; match your total score to a random number. You will lose the game if your total score surpasses the random number.
  • The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on the crystal of your choice.
  • The value of the crystals and random number will change after the completion each game.
